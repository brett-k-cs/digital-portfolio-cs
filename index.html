Welcome to my Digital Portfolio!

Why Do I have a Portfolio?

The simple answer, it is required. However, it also important to have a digital portfolio because it can act as a resume. Employers often want an example of what you can do and what you have done. Many software engineers & front-end developers have amazing digital portfolios that show off their skills. My favorite is Bruno Simon's portfolio. He uses ThreeJS to make an amazing and interactive portfolio. It shows off his previous projects and his current projects which builds his credibility. This is why a portfolio is important to me. I want to have a place that I can link to that shows off all of my education, my previous projects, my previous employers, and any other experience.

This portfolio lives at https://brett-k-cs.github.io/digital-portfolio-cs/
